Our Story

Founded in 2015, Khatra Coffee began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional coffee experiences to our community. We source our beans from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring every sip supports both quality and ethical practices.

Our master roasters bring decades of expertise, carefully crafting each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of every origin. From light, floral Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to bold, chocolatey Colombian Supremo, our diverse selection caters to every palate.
